Vision

To develop SDCMT as an excellent education Centre that empowers students to become successful leaders, entrepreneurs and technocrats in a holistic environment.

Mission

SDCMT fosters academic and career success through the development of critical thinking, effective communication, creativity, and cultural awareness in a safe, accessible and affordable learning environment. We are committed for creating and disseminating path breaking knowledge, concepts, and tools which advance the understanding and practice of management and technology.
