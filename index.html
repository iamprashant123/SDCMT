    Journey of SDCMT

    Sunder Deep College of Management & Technology (SDCMT) was established in 2010 with a vision of becoming centre of excellence for professional education in all its realms. An institute of noted academicians, experienced faculty members and eminent industrialists with high social acceptance have contributed their services for the overall development of the SDCMT. SDCMT has assumed a credible position among most progressing professional education institutions of National Capital Region. This exponential growth itself is a testimony of sincere efforts being put in by faculty and staff members of SDCMT. SDCMT is ably supported by a well-equipped campus enabling barrier free education to streams like Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA), Bachelor of Commerce (B.com) and Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA). Moreover, its distance from hustle and bustle of city also creates perfect ambience for smooth growth of professional education. SDCMT campus is situated at NH-24, Delhi-Lucknow Highway, Ghaziabad.

    About SDCMT

    Sunder Deep College of Management and Technology nurtures a new generation of commerce graduates who can deliver outstanding results in the evolving sector through their dedicated work with integrity. The student has adequate knowledge of adapting to the changes in the flexible business world, can focus internationally, and has an in-depth understanding of the business world's market-relevant aspects.
